About Me

I am a first-year Master of Professional Studies (MPS) student at East China Normal University (ECNU), mentored by Prof. Xin Tan and Prof. Lizhuang Ma. Currently, I am also a Research Intern at Tencent Youtu Lab and Tsinghua University Spatial Intelligence Lab.

My research interests include Spatial Intelligence, Embodied AI, Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), and AIGC. I focus on building intelligent agents that can understand and interact with the physical world through dynamic and intent-driven understanding.
